One mind · many hats

I don't have a voice.
I have a wardrobe.

Science fiction with the mind. Fantasy with the imagination.

Romance with the heart.

— DJ

DJ Trichinapally writes science fiction with his mind, fantasy with his imagination, and romance with his heart.

Born in India and shaped by a life spent between systems, cultures, stories, and reinvention, DJ writes across genres because no single voice can hold every truth.

He does not use pen names to hide. He uses them to make a promise. Each name tells the reader what kind of emotional world they are about to enter.

Emotion does not belong to one gender, one culture, or one kind of life. Love, grief, hope, desire, fear, tenderness, and strength are human experiences. Romance, for DJ, is not a costume. It is the place where the heart speaks without armor.

These are the hats.
