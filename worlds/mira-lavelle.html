About Mira Lavelle

The author behind the romance

Mira Lavelle writes love stories you can feel in your chest — formidable heroines, slow-burn tension, and the quiet, dangerous places where memory and desire overlap. Atmosphere you can smell, secrets that cost something, and endings that earn their ache.

For readers of A.S. Byatt, Sarah Penner, and anyone who likes a love story with a locked room in it.
