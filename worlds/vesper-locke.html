About Vesper Locke

The author behind the SF

Vesper Locke writes science fiction about the machinery of control and the people who refuse to be optimized away. Expect precise, propulsive prose, hard moral stakes, ensemble casts you'd run a kill-lane for, and climaxes built to feel both enormous and human.

If you love Red Rising, The Expanse, and spectacle turned into resistance — this is your world.
