About Eveline Cross

The author behind the doorways

Eveline Cross writes portal-fantasy adventure about thresholds — the bridges, doors, and seams where one world spills into another, and the unlikely people who learn to walk between them. Expect fast wonder, real peril, found family, and worlds you'll want to stay lost in.

For readers who never stopped looking for the door at the back of the wardrobe.
