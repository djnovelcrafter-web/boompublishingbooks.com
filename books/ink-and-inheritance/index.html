A Dual-Timeline Gothic Romantic-Suspense Series

Ink & Inheritance

A rare-book authenticator keeps finding documents that were never meant to be true — forged death notices, altered ledgers, names written carefully over other names. Each was made by a woman with no other way to survive an unjust law… and each, somehow, seems to have been waiting for her to find it.

The Series

Two books, one buried truth

Read in order. Each book is a complete love story and mystery — but together they trace a single forged inheritance across two centuries, and the women who refused to let the record erase them.

What to Expect

If you love gothic suspense

Dual Timeline

1886 and the present braided together, each timeline carrying a secret the other needs to survive.

Forgery as Survival

Women erased by unjust laws who wrote themselves back into existence — one careful, dangerous document at a time.

Slow-Burn Romance

Tension you can feel in the margins. The kind of longing that makes the danger sharper.

A Mystery That Pays Off

Every clue is load-bearing. Nothing is decoration. The ending re-reads the beginning.

For readers of The Binding (Bridget Collins), The Thirteenth Tale (Diane Setterfield), and The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue (V.E. Schwab) — atmosphere, secrets, and a romance that aches.
