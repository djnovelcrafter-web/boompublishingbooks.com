What to Expect

If you love costly magic

Magic With a Price

Power is never free here. Every crossing, every awakening, every rescue costs something that can’t be bought back.

Found Family

A pre-med genius, a synesthetic artist, and a girl who became a door. The people who stay are the whole point.

Two Worlds at War

Earth and Aethora, bridges and councils and a zealot who wants to retune reality. Stakes that scale across three books.

A Heroine Worth Saving

Lina is brave, funny, and increasingly dangerous — and the trilogy never lets you forget what it costs her to be the key.
