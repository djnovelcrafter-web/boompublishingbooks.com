The Storyteller

One author, three names

DJ Trichinapally writes science fiction with his mind, fantasy with his imagination, and romance with his heart.

The pen names are not disguises — they are promises. Vesper Locke means cold, kinetic science fiction. Eveline Cross means portal-fantasy adventure with heart. Mira Lavelle means romance and gothic suspense where love, longing, and secrets refuse to stay quiet.

Stories don't belong to genres. They belong to people.

Born in India and shaped by a life spent between systems, cultures, stories, and reinvention, DJ writes across genres because no single voice can hold every truth.
