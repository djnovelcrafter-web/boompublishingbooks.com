The Storyteller

One author, three names

I write across three worlds under three names. When you pick up a Vesper Locke book you're getting cold, kinetic science fiction. Eveline Cross means portal-fantasy adventure with heart. Mira Lavelle is for atmospheric romance and gothic suspense.

The pen names aren't disguises — they're promises. Each one tells you exactly what kind of story you're about to fall into, so you always know which world you're stepping through the door of. This page is where all three worlds meet.

Three names. Three worlds. One storyteller — and a new door always opening.

Why three names? Because personality isn't who you are — it's what you wear. The pen names are hats: the futurist, the world-builder, the romantic — and the philosopher and architect behind them all.
